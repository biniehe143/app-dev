## Summary  
*Stranger Things* is a sci-fi thriller series set in the 1980s town of Hawkins, Indiana, where a group of kids uncover secret experiments, supernatural forces, and a parallel dimension called the Upside Down. The story centers on Eleven, a girl with telekinetic powers, who escapes from a lab and helps her friends battle mysterious creatures threatening their world.

## Why I Love It  
*Stranger Things* is a great series because it shows the bond between friends — **unity, love, humor, nostalgia, and strong characters**.  
Its storyline gives viewers vibes about the **80s**, **family**, **love**, **friendship**, and **acceptance**.  
It doesn’t just excite — it teaches you to be **courageous**, **to trust**, and **to wait**.

>*"Friends don't lie."*
